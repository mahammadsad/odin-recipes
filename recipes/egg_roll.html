Egg Roll

About Egg Roll

Egg Roll is a popular street food loved for its delicious combination of crispy paratha, fluffy egg coating, and flavorful fillings. Originating from Kolkata, this quick and satisfying snack is enjoyed by people of all ages. It is commonly served as a grab-and-go meal and can be customized with vegetables, sauces, and spices according to personal preference.

Why People Love Egg Roll?

Egg Rolls are popular because they are simple to prepare, affordable, and incredibly tasty. The crispy outer layer paired with the soft egg filling creates a perfect balance of textures. Whether enjoyed as a snack, lunch, or evening meal, an Egg Roll provides a flavorful and filling experience that keeps people coming back for more.

Ingredients Needed

  • 2 parathas or flatbreads
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 small cucumber, julienned
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (optional)
  • Chaat masala (optional)

How to Make Egg Roll?

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and add salt and black pepper.
  2. Beat the eggs thoroughly until well combined.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and cook the paratha until lightly golden on both sides.
  4. Pour a small amount of oil into the pan.
  5. Add the beaten egg to the pan and immediately place the cooked paratha on top of the egg.
  6. Press gently so the egg sticks to the paratha.
  7. Cook for 1–2 minutes until the egg is fully set.
  8. Flip and cook the other side for another minute.
  9. Remove the egg-coated paratha from the pan.
  10. Spread ketchup and chili sauce evenly over the surface.
  11. Add sliced onion, cucumber, green chili, and coriander leaves.
  12. Sprinkle chaat masala if desired.
  13. Roll the paratha tightly into a wrap.
  14. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Egg Roll.
